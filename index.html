  • Sound of Metal
    Directed by Darius Marder, Sound of Metal is an empathetic character study on outgrowing our surroundings and dealing with a life-changing event. Riz Ahmed gives his best performance of his career as Ruben, a drummer who is going deaf due to his own decisions. Ahmed plays this role with restraint and low-key actions that makes it all the more powerful and genuine. Marder and Ahmed have come together to make a refined character study that doesn't feel cheap or gimmicky. The movie thrives in silence with the movements and facial expressions of Ahmed telling the story. Read More
